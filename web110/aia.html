Audience Issues, Concerns and Needs Approach
Potential clients - People ages from the early 20s up to late 70s who are willing to travel for fun on a low budget, and need guidance with what to do in the cities in order to make the most out of their trip.
  • Looking for tips that are not mentioned in most websites.
  • Want detailed information and suggestions.
  • Clients who reach out to the website hoping to find guidance for the whole trip, and prefer the option of having support.
  • Create a monthly subscription option to keep clients informed.
  • Add small descriptions and appealing images to social media websites linking back to the website.
  • A website that is clean, easy to navigate and mobile-friendly.
Students - Students who are planning a trip and wish to know more about things to do with friends. They might not be experienced travelers but looks forward to gaining knowledge about it. Clients who want to travel for a specific event hosted by school/college and then want to go sightseeing.
  • Need content that focuses on young adults traveling by themselves or in groups without the company of a family member.
  • Need simple and clear navigation.
  • Prefer to know about fun and free things to do.
  • Use User-Generated Content - publish content from clients on our social media account with their permission.
  • Advertise the website with Google Adwords and Facebook Ads.
  • Create a dedicated communication channel for clients in order to build relationships and respond quickly to online customer contact.
Partners - Established companies who want to become sponsors of the website in order to attract clients, such as hotels, airline companies, and travel agencies. They would all benefit from having a partnership with our website.
  • The website needs to show professionalism and focus on the clients' needs.
  • Reliable partner that keeps in touch and provides support.
  • Want to offer services to vacationers.
  • Have a simple, lightweight database for better user experience.
  • Advertise the website with Google Adwords and Facebook Ads.
  • Collaborate with bloggers, news and/or travel writers in our region.
