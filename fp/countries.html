Places Visited

This website was made with a purpose to share my own experience around the world. I’ve been to 52 countries and explored six continents. I’ve seen several castles in Europe including the ones in London, in Denmark and Dracula’s castle in Transylvania. Furthermore, Archaeological sites such as The Parthenon in Athens, Easter Islands, Maia site in Mexico and Belize, Rome and its marvelous coliseum and many others. I’ve had a lot of contact with nature, under water, in the Amazon or in the desert when I rode a camel in Dubai. I have swum with stingrays, dolphins, and for several times I went snorkeling joined by an array of colorful fishes and huge turtles. I want to share all of my knowledge with everyone and hopefully increase people’s interest in traveling.

Continents Explored

Africa

Africa is the world's second-largest and second-most populous continent, after Asia. It comprises 54 countries, countless different landscapes, and a myriad of unique ethnic groups. You can go on a Safari, relax on a beach, discover history, and make a difference volunteering.

Asia

Asia is the largest and most populous continent on earth. You can visit the Taj Mahal, feel the awe of history in the Buddhist cave temples of Dambulla and fly over the Himalayas at sunrise, and do much much more.

Australia

Australia’s cities are full of wonderful parks and gardens, excellent markets, and brilliant art galleries and museums, and in Sydney you can tour the iconic Sydney Opera House and the Sydney Harbour Bridge. Sun and surf are also available in abundance

Europe

Europe, in particular ancient Greece and ancient Rome, was the birthplace of Western civilisation. There's so much to explore in Europe when it comes to historical sites.

North America

The continent is richly endowed with natural resources, including great mineral wealth, vast forests, immense quantities of fresh water, and some of the world’s most fertile soils. These have allowed North America to become one of the most economically developed regions in the world.

South America

South America is the wilder of the Americas, and a continent of superlatives. The world's biggest rainforest and the largest river (Amazon), the Andes, remote islands, heavenly beaches, wide deserts, Patagonia, Iguaçu Falls, as well as several other breathtaking natural attractions.
