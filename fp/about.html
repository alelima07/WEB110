About

Hey there, my name is Jaqueline Witthinrich, I'm a Brazilian model, photographer, travel and lifestyle blogger obsessed with exploring, cruelty-free beauty, fashion and of course, planning new adventures around the globe. And I've been stumbling my way around the world for six years and counting.

In my spare time, you will find me scrapbooking, checking a new vegan restaurant or Broadway show in town; watching F.R.I.E.N.D.S, Sex and The City, or some popular reality TV show. I also love taking photos of people and hanging out with my family, friends and my husband.

Through my travels, I had the opportunity of seeing wonderful places I love, you can see some of them in this website! If you believe we are a good fit, make sure to contact me for more information on how we can collaborate and create something great together!

If you are a traveler lover like me, make sure to leave a comment telling me what is your favorite thing about visiting new places! Meanwhile, here is a moment of me exploring the south of Brazil! Hope this website is useful to you and I hope to see you back soon!

Xoxo,
